About NewsBits India

NewsBits India is a personal side project. I got tired of opening five different news apps and scrolling past clickbait just to find out what actually happened that day, so I built something simpler: short, bullet-point headlines from a handful of trustworthy Indian and international publishers, updated automatically throughout the day.

The idea is to let you catch up on the day's news in under a minute. Each headline links straight back to the original publisher's website, where you can read the full story. NewsBits India does not rewrite, republish, or claim ownership of any article content — it simply curates and links to the source.

This is still a work in progress, built and maintained by one person in their spare time. If you spot something broken or have a suggestion, the contact link in the footer reaches me directly.
