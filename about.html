About NewsBits India

NewsBits India is a free news digest that collects the latest headlines from trusted Indian and international publishers and presents them as short, scannable bullet points — so you can catch up on what's happening in under a minute.

The feed is refreshed automatically throughout the day. Each headline links back to the original publisher's website, where you can read the full story. NewsBits India does not rewrite, republish, or claim ownership of any article content — we simply curate and link.
