What this site is

NewsBits India is a personal news aggregator. It automatically pulls headlines from publicly available RSS feeds published by third-party news organizations and links back to each publisher's original article. Alongside each headline, the site shows a short summary that is either (a) a brief excerpt taken directly from the publisher's own RSS feed, or (b) an original summary written by an AI system, based only on the publisher's headline and RSS snippet, to make the digest quicker to read. Either way, this site does not independently report, fact-check, or verify any news content.
